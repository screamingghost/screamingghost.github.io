👻 We revived a Screaming Goat! 👻

ScreamingGoat 🐐 was a beloved member of infosec.exchange, an ActivityPub exchange. They operated a site on infosec.press, which in turn was blog hosted and federated by infosec.exchange

This site is an attempt to codify their wisdom via GitHub Pages. This format has trade offs against the federation model, but it's an easy way to open up this content and perhaps allow some additional related content to authored.

I am Bees 🐝🐝🐝 - I migrated this account after a brief internet friendship with Goat, and appreciation of their APT and vuln knowledge. I intend to further some of this research, to worship the memory of ScreamingGoat (the ScreamingGhost 👻) by releasing China APT related data and tools with this site.

That's all! Feel free to contact me via infosec.exchange or this GitHub repository. Happy hunting!
